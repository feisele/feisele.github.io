Florian Eisele

Postdoctoral researcher in Representation Theory

City, University of London

Biography

Since October 2015 I am a post-doctoral researcher working in the representation theory group at City, University of London. I previously held a position at Vrije Universiteit Brussel in Belgium. I completed my PhD on “Group rings over the p-adic integers” at RWTH Aachen University in 2012. An up-to-date academic CV is available here.

Interests

  • Modular representation theory
  • Representations of Artin algebras
  • Integral and p-adic group rings
  • τ-tilting theory
  • Unit groups
  • Finite group theory
  • Constructive methods & computer algebra
